“Flowers will come again with Spring.”

Joy’s a cheat there’s no denying; Love hath too a wandering wing; Still ’tis useless sighing Rather list to Hope replying, “Flowers will come again with Spring.”

Fame’s a liar – Beauty fading – Hope itself may plant a sting; Still forbear, forbear upbraiding – Rather list to her persuading “Flowers will come again with Spring.”

Friendship turns, itself denying – Yea, itself! the heart to wring Still even though all faith be dying, Ever list to Hope replying, “Flowers will come again with Spring”
