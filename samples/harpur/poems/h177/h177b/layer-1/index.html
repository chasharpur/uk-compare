“Flowers will come again with Spring.”

Joy’s a cheat there’s no denying; Love hath too a wandering wing; Still ’tis useless sighing Rather list to Hope replying, “Flowers will come again with Spring.”

Fame’s a deceiver – Beauty fading – Hope itself may plant a sting; Still forbear, forbear upbraiding – Rather list to her persuading “Flowers will come again with Spring.”

Even Friendship turns, decrying – Its own friend the heart to wring Still even though its faith be dying, Still list to Hope replying, “Flowers will come again with Spring”
