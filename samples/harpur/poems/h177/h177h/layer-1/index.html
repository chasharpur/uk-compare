A Portion of Charles Harpur’s Wild Bee of Australia.

Additional Stanza for Hope’s Philosophy.

[...] All is dream-like; all is fleeting;— All that time and sense can bring: Then comes death!—’tis vain entreating! Rather list to Hope repeating: Heaven is one Eternal spring! And through the midst there rolls a river In which to bathe is joy forever— Hark! I hear it murmuring.
