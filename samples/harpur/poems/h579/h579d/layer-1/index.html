The Temperance Movement.

A Power is stirring—a broad Light has shone Amid the Nations;—in the wilderness Of the world’s social horror and distress Heralding Temperance as the Baptist John Announced the Christ. Amazed upon her throne Built up of skulls that were in life not less Than temples of great spirits—behold Excess Blinks in its rays, and feels her empire gone! And Ignorance and Crime—each brutal Vice That brands the brow with shame and steels the heart, Are starting from their lairs in human sties, Like felons scared, and gathering to begone: Even as the Fiend Gods of the pagan earth Trooped hellward at the Babe of Bethlehem’s birth.
