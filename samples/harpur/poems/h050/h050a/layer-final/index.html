The bold eyed Mariner

I knew a bold-eyed Mariner, who held Opinions strange and startling, yet thr[o]ugh which A wild furies wisdom looked, or seemed to look; And he would give them utterance with a will That urged them, in their massive import, forth, Ensue what might, as thunder clouds are swept Flamingly onward by a Hurricane!
