IV.

The Cock of the Walk.

That self-sufficient, self-reliant, doughty Martin mar- vellous— That plucky, plaguy, petulant, pugnacious indi- vidual, Said to the Bar of many a star, so high and pure and Darvallous, I am your Master—mark you that! and to the trial bid you all. And truth to say, that company of Gods, or Godward- growing men, But cackled like so many hens against his Cock-like crowing then.
