SONNETS DEDICATED TO AUSTRALIAN SENATORS.

NO. III.

WHO’S THE MAN?

A Doctor! One whose mouth is filled with slang, Not Scripture, such as a flash “Gully raker” Might use, did he his stubborn herd harangue! The same who held it murderously wrang To hang in any case the prison breaker Who brained his guard merely to ‘scape from limbo— (Being primed therein by a Quixotic quaker, Who still in morals will be jumping Jim Crow!) The same who for his scurril venom’s sake Is indirectly bonussed by a crew Of public cheats, that win him so to stake His Saintship in their cause.—I say not who This Doctor is—I only say that wrang Rhymes with our Senatorial name of————
