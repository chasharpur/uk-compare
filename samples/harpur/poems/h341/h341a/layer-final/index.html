Ola Sam Terry.

Old Sam Terry went to a Church meeting Where he met from the parsons a great deal of greeting, But as he stood bowing on his old gouty toes there, All on a sudden he made a great snort they say, Alarming the belles and the beaux there! Let a great f–t too;—then fell all amort that day: Dying the next, and going—God knows where!
