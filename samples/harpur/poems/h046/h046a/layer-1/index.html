Bishop Berkeley.

I love him mightily, and fain would know Whether he’d have me, or not have me, go; Yet read, to find him too recondite, since His logic puzzles so, it don’t convince. So wide his arguments, that we suspect it Of abberations, though we can’t detect it, Or that it dives so deep into the main Of Reason, that it ne’er comes up again.
