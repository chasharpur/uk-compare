X Speak the Truth.

Let the truth be always told, Despite of loss, or bonds, or death! It fits not that the bold Should breathe dishonest breath.

Truth in word is first the way For all who would in deed be true: 51 And none should dare to say What he in real do.

‘Tis the sole heroic plan— The one strict line ‘twixt low and high! For it unmans a Man Only to think a lie.
