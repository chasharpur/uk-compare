Sacred to the Memory of Charles Chidley Harpur, who departed this life on the 2nd. of March 1867. Aged 13 years.

Human Affection would fain have held him For ever encaged in the appearances of Earth: But Divine Love had higher claims upon his Spirit, And called him early to the Realities of Heaven.

The Lord gave, and the Lord hath taken away: blessed be the name of the Lord.
