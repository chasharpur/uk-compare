Impromptu Stanzas.

Addressed to a Collection of the Author’s earliest Poems, while committing the Manuscripts to the flames.

Ye records of Visions that fled Before the inclement World’s terrible strife; Of bright Joys and pure Sorrows dispread Like flowers to be nipt in the blooming, or shed Like dew in excess, o’er the morning of Life!
