WORDS.

Words are deeds. The words we hear May revolutionise or rear A mighty state. The words we read May be a spiritual deed Excelling any fleshly one, As much as the celestial sun Transcends a bonfire, made to throw A light upon some raree-show. A simple proverb tagged with rhyme May colour half the course of time; The pregnant saying of a sage May influence every coming age; A song in its effects may be More glorious than Thermopylæ, And many a lay that schoolboys scan A nobler feat than Inkerman.
