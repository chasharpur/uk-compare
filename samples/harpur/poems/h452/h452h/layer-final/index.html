Records of Romantic Passion.

There’s a rare spirit of feeling that may be Ingathered only by the chastened dreams Of constant hearts. Hence, where the ministry Of beautiful nature hath enhanced the themes Of some Petrarchian mind, whose story gleams Within the Past like a moon-silvered sea; Or where the fortunes of True Love, once free, Grey Interest hath encaged in iron schemes, Or stirred the winds of Ruin till they drove In tempest over them;—there ever after, The very airs that whisper to the grove, The echo’s mystery, and the streamlet’s laughter, Savor of passion, and with subtlest wafture Infuse suggestions to Romantic Love.
