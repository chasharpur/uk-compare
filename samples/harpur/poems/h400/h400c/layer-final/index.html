“Property is Funded Talent.”

To hold that pelf is funded nous Is just about as true indeed, As that a nit’s an actual louse In virtue of its breed! Whereas ’tis futile, unless Fate Dry nurse it into louse-estate, And then, even, (putting it for pelf) It ne’er may fund a nit itself! And thus is shown though through a louse, The unfundability of Nous, Which, could we but entail it, were As indestructible as air.
