“Property is Funded Talent.”

To hold that pelf is funded nous Is just about as true indeed, As that a nit‘s an actual louse In virtue of its breed; Whereas ’tis nothing,—unless Fate Through means superior to itself, Dry-nurse it into louse-estate;— Just such a nit is pelf! And such a nit would pelf remain, Unquickened by the power of brain; Whereas, if truly put for wit, ‘Twould, spite of Fate, its lousehood gain And in its turn self-fund a nit. And thus I show, though through a louse, The unfundability of Nous.
