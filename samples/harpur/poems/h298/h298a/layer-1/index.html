Memo Begun 22 May 1867

Obituary Lines

My dear dear Charley, can it be that thou Art gone from me for ever? A I sit Amid these forest shadows that now fall Upon thy early Grave; and think of all The love & joy that has between us been So late, and during all thy little life— Thy thirteen years of sonhood, it is hard To realise the solemn fact; that thou art now Art now in the Eternal World—as reft away, In wild moment from thy Father’s l heart, Yea gone as utterly, from his lonely side As thy Boy’s shadow, once so often there Hath vanished out of Time Thy dear intelligence from his lonely side- Gone in a moment—gone as utterly As hath thy shadow, once so often there— Thy slim Boy-shadow, leaving him alas!
