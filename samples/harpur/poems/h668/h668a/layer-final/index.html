XIX.

WAR.

He who in battle slays his fellow Man, Without first having duly weighed the cause Of quarrel, and derived the approval thence Of his own heart as to the part therein By him to be sustained,—he is, indeed, Strictly a Murderer! And though this stern Truth Should sound distastefully to many—those Especially who trade in national madness And brutal glory; magnifying so A rag inscribed with some stale jugglery Heraldic, far more than they honor God! Yet, what then? Is not this great World grown grey In woe if not in weal? and surely now ‘Tis time the voice of Truth, in all things, should Be lifted into fearless utterance! On this head, had its simple test been taught From the beginning—O, what seas of blood Had so been spared! what withering desolations, What infamies, prevented!

In the roll Of History,—who that hath a mind to think, And heart to feel, can read her constant tale Of royal trickery to unholy wars Still leading—wholesale human butcheries! Nor curse the moral blindness that hath given The Nations hitherto to bow them down And god their very Scourges! Yea, until Evil hath become their Good, and each a school Merely of rival Inhumanities Called Heroes, and great Captains of their age, And heaven-born Ministers who seek no Heaven But human power;—all schooled to that extreme, Wherein the identities of Right and Wrong Are elbowed from the conscience by fast crowds Of kindless schemes, established on the Fact, That national mischief is the epic road To national distinction;—thence to wade So far in Murder even, that the lets Of natural piety, with the grosser shapes Of horror and remorse, which set some bound To vulgar Violence, are swallowed up In the dark scope of their perverted Lives, By one all-blanking flood of crime at last: As erst each landmark of the Earth was lost In the blind growth of the prevailing Deluge! And hence it is, their terrible careers Completed, that such blood-damned Hirelings die In a benumbed incertitude of heart That looks like peace! The military Fiend, Napoleon, hence,—a wave-bound prisoner, And perishing piecemeal on a desolate rock,— Escaped the sense of blood, and saw the bulk Of Death come onward calmly as a Saint.
