THE LAST HOPE.

Uprising from the bed of care, Which through the night no slumber gave, Oh, where is rest? asked wan despair, And hope low answered—In the grave!

There scorn forgetteth to deride, And those she injured to complain; There foemen lay them side by side, And sundered friends are joined again.

Charles Harpur.

Singleton, 1st November, 1842.
