IV.

Song.

O Love is passing sweet, When we meet With a golden-hearted Maid, Not afraid To glory in her part, And love with all her heart. Till it beat—till it beat To the nature-warbled tune of Love is sweet!

It is like a sunset sky To the eye; It is like a odourous wind To the mind; It is like a fervent roll Of music to the soul; And we cry—and we cry Let us always love, or failing, Let us die!
