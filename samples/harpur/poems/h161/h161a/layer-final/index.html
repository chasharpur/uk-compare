The Great Fish of the see (sea)

Your Jesuit, in the Pope’s infallibility Can see the only barrier of utility ‘Gaint Christian discord, heresy and schism; Whilst a state gownsman of the English people Would base his lordly Church from crypt to steeple Less on the Bible than the Catechism. And thus for the same spiritual trout Both angle, though with different tackle out: A huge monopoly in the cure of souls, Fat masters, and blind worshippers in shoals.
