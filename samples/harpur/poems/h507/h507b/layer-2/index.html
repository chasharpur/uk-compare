VII.

Hector, advancing, carried through the van His equal-curving shield. As when a fierce Pernicious star, in red appearance, glares An the parted clouds;—now seen—then hid; Now shining bright—then dwindling dark, betwixt The closing gaps of gloom: so Hector strode Giving his orders through the ranks, and shone Now ’mid the foremost, then amongst the last, Glittering all over in his burnished mail, Bright as a bolt of Jove.
