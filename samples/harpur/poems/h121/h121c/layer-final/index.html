Evasion.

Oh! wronged as I am, if I had but a dagger, Roared Abel the schoolmaster, gasping for breath! 395 I would drive it right home to my heart, and then stagger Just like a stuck pig, into darkness and death! But a wag at his elbow said,—struck with a plan How to turn into gas the self-threat of the bragger: There’s a knife on the table there—up with it, man! Fiend, away! was the answer;—a knife‘s not a dagger!
