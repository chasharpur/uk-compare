Song

Oh Love it is a pleasant song That wakens in the Spring, When days are waxing bright and long And bowers are blossoming; To strengthen all the powers that bless, And gladden all employ, To bring a joy to tenderness, A tenderness to joy.

In the warm influence of the skies It anthems Beauty’s birth, Or raineth thence to fertilize 57 The bridals of the earth: And then, if measureful and strong It cometh to our door, Oh, love it is a pleasant song To cheer us evermore.
