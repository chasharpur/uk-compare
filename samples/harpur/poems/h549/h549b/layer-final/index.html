[What Ne]xt?

[Bardolph. Why, sir, for my] part, I say, the gentleman had [drunk himself out of his five sen]tences, x x x and being fap, x x [(he’s) conslusions passed the] careires (proper pace.)

[Surely some Spirit of F]olly that much hates man, Was prevalent in Windsor, (in the shape Of high-drugg’d rum ’tis likely) when Bill Ape Was therein chosen to go forth a Statesman! Yes, and what’s more, Bill, you were surely fap When weening first, so much Acrastus flatters, Your genius fitted you for public matters Almost as well as for a public Tap! What though your Sheep were numberless indeed, Even richer yet were many a greater gaby! Nor would you therefore a right National stay be! Fine Flocks are well—but more Australia’s need Asks now of Providence a nobler breed Of Men, Sir; men!—as unlike you as may be!
