Extreme Comparisons.

For shame, snooks! What? pretend to me That you are generous and free? You, who are both in word in As mean as—yes, a needy lord,! Or a Grand Vizier of the East! When did you but one liberal act? And are you not as fond, in fact, Of money—as a priest?
