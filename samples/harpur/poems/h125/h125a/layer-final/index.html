Extreme Comparisons.

For shame, snooks! What? pretend to me That you are generous and free? You, who are both in deed and word As mean as—yes, a needy lord,! Why, you pretensive beast, When did you but one liberal act? And are you not as fond, in fact, Of money—as a priest? Or Parson? (which word would be usable As aptly here, were rhyme excusable.)
