Reasons for a Red Nose.

Ask the Doctor why Mrs. G’s nose is so red, And brandy‘s the word (with a wink) that is said; But the lady herself vows she verily thinks ‘Tis because of the great deal of water she drinks: Nor is either quite right, or quite wrong, in the matter, Since the truth lies in too much of the latter With too much of the first in’t—brandy and water!
