The Spirit of Love. ᴓ X not

When broad o’er her hill-top Hangs Eve’s glowing star, A spirit of beauty comes, As from afar; To dream through the gloaming’s Heart-hushing degrees, And breathe like a vow In the odour-sweet breeze:

To mellow the echoes That bleat from the hills Where the Shepherds are folding Their flocks by the rills; In the wild woods to deepen The dove’s gentle voice— To live in all life, In all gladness rejoice.

It is breathing around, It is glowing above, 24 That spirit of beauty— The Spirit of Love.
