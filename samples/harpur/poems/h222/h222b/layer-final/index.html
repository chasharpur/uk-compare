Lines suggested by a newly invented sort of pickle-dish.

Well done, Improvement! thou dost even outrace Our most fastidious, most fantastic wishes, Since that for serving up with better grace Even pickles, we have now these special dishes. 402 But while I scan them with admiring glance They breed a thought that stings me like a prickle, Asking Improvement, if by any chance Pickle-dish makers ever eat a pickle?

“A pickle? no: those pungents, pricing dearly, Are for their pastors and their masters merely:” Replies Improvement; and to stave off schism, The well-fed hussy quotes Church Catechism.

Then in the sanctuary seeketh she Her Christ—Political Economy; Who hails her in with his divinest smile, And bids her rest upon her wheel awhile: Biding her final, perfect state—a day When Capital shall take a god’s full style And buy the world, and lord it, until they Who raise our corn shall live themselves on vile, Refuse and garbage—or, it may be, hay! Save when hog-fattened in some splendid stye Built like a Crystal Palace, arched and high; Hog-fattened for their oil—the oil sublime Reserved for engines in that golden time Which, as it looms upon the Muse’s eye, I thus fore-pickle in a grateful Rhyme.
