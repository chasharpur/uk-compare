A Critic at the top of his Ladder.

192

Every sect will have its canters; Every stage will [unclear] have its ranters; Every craft will have its mystic 193 Doctors, greyly cabalistic; Every art will have its riddlers Jargoning like drunken fiddlers: Whence it happens, one Frank Fowler, With his constant cheek-by-jowler, Namby-Pamby, for his very Clever under secretary, Is the self-installed Grand Vizier Of the Doggreldom we quiz here.
