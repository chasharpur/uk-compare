A Critic at the Top of his Ladder.

Every Sect will have its ranters, Every Stage will have its ranters; Every Craft will have its mystic Doctors, greyly cabalistic; Every Art will have its riddlers Jargoning like drunken fidle fiddlers: Whence it happens, one Frank Fowler With his constant cheek-by-jowler, 394 Namby Pamby, for his very Clever Under Secretary, Is the self-installed Grand Vizier Of the Doggereldom we quiz here.
