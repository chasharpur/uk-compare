III

A Pastoral Picture.

All simple sights of Rural Life to me Are fresh or beautiful. Look down the stream, Where, under the broad shade, a dappled Cow Stoops her meek head over the grassy brink, And then, with rapid and strong gulphs, indraws 3a The pure cool lymph: and though at length sufficed, Still over it she keeps her neck declined And breathes her herby breath upon the water, Ruffling the image of herself below, As there, with pendent head and dappled sides, It stands reversed: herself and shadow both Patient expressions of her utter comfort.
