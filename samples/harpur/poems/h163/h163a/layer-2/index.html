The great Gun of the Patriotic Fund, brought to a queer Test.

Jack Sham, with many duties lying Under his very nose unfound, And after much internal sighing, To think of other Shams out vying Thumped down fifty pound! Several more, with Mort the thrifty, Gave five times fifty; One Bank down thundered Twice that—five hundred! But to its store Dan Cooper flung a thousand! And should a beggar fling a Louse and No more, I wonder which would be The greater Patriot—Dan or He?
