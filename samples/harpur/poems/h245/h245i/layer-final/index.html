21

“Yes”

My Soul is saying like a star, My heart is happier than a bird, And all to hear through fortunes jar One promising Word.

A sound as simple as the low Quick sliding gurgle of a rill, And yet of power to overflow A world with blissful will!

I feel as though the very air Was breathed from the heart of Love; As pleasure in the suns bright lair Sat brooding like a dove!

A billow of the sunny sea A cloudlet of the summer sky, How wide is their felicity— So wildly blest am I!

22

Oh Beauty, though one little word, What power is there to raise, to bless! O Love, a seraph’s voice is heard In thy confiding “Yes”.
