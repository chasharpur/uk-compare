Love the Idealiser.

I.

My soul is raying like a star! My heart is happier than a bird! And all to hear, through Fortune’s jar, One promissory word.

A sound as simple as the low Quick sliding gurgle of a rill, And yet of power to overflow A world with blissful will.

Yea, such as makes even lowliest things That dull and tuneless seemed before, Mount as it were on lark-like wings, And warble as they soar.

I feel as if all life and might Had started on a loftier course; As if all passion and delight Were deepened at the source.

I feel as if the very air Was breathen from the heart of Love; As Pleasure in the sun’s bright lair Sat brooding like a dove.

A fruit tree fleeced with blossoms bright— A fountain in the morning shine— How exquisite is their delight? So exquisite is mine.

A billow of the sunny sea— A cloudlet of the sunny sky— How wide is their felicity? So widely blest am I.

O Beauty, even through a word, What powers are thine, to raise and bless! O Love, a seraph’s voice is heard In thy confiding Yes!
