I.

GOD’S MAN.

(Being a Paraphrase of the 15th Psalm.)

Who before the Lord shall stand? Who shall sit at God’s right hand?

He whose path of life is straight; He to whom the Truth’s estate Is greater than a crownëd King’s; He who hates all whisperings Against his neighbour, speaking aye Only what his heart would say, And that aloud in language clear Of all disguise, above all fear.

He to whom all guile is guile, All goodness good, all vileness vile; Whose promise made in very deed, Is sacred, let what will succeed; He who takes not usury; he, Who though a world the bribe should be, In judgment never would consent To cast the hapless innocent.

He who is thus of God approved, Never, never shall be moved.
