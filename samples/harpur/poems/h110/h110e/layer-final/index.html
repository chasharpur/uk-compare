Emblems X

A Streamlet is a bright and beauteous creature In some wide desert where it keeps a part Of each wayfarer’s heart; The Star of Evening is a gracious feature, Instinct as ‘twere with all the love that eyes Have looked through at the skies.

And hence one emblems in its beauteous being, The memory of a love that in our heart Flowed gratefully apart; The other to our spiritual seeing, The perpetuity of that love’s white grace Yet in some heavenly place.
