[ORIGINAL.]

EMBLEMS.

BY CHARLES HARPUR, ESQ.

A Streamlet is a bright and beautiful creature, In some wide desert, where it keeps a part Of each way-farer’s heart; The Star of evening is a gracious feature, Yearning, as ‘twere, will all the love that eyes Have looked through at the skies:

And hence, one emblems in its beautiful being, The memory of a Love that in my heart Flows gratefully apart; The other, to my spiritual seeing, The perpetuity of its white grace Yet in some better place.
