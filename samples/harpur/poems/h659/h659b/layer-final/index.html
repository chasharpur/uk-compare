The value of Frankness queerly tested.

Dan, turned insolvent, blurted Blood and ‘ounds! Why Harry, should our friendship now be sundered? If I have diddled you of twenty pounds, What’s that?—I’ve diddled Roger of a hundred! And this excuse, though made without compunction, Had, strange to say, a mollifying unction.

So once a French girl, being given to know Her hands were very filthy, with a sweet Excusive look, admitted they were so, But added—If you only saw my feet! And her excuse too had, (I do not doubt it) Being frank, a dirty sort of charm about it.
