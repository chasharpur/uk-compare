V.

An Infallible Soporific.

Another night is passed—and yet You tell me, Friend, no sleep you get, Do what you will! You plough, you reap, You journey,—and yet fail to sleep! You try all soporifics,—yet The deuce a wink of sleep you get! And in a desperate case like this, You ask my best advice? Here ’tis: You know one Brainworm—well but stay! So blue you need not look indeed! He wrote a Book, and mind, I say, Do you just get that Book to read.
