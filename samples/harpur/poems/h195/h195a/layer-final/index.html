AN INFALLIBLE SOPORIFIC.

Another night is past, and yet You tell me, Friend, no sleep you get, Do what you will! you plough, you reap, You journey,—and yet fail to sleep! You try all Soporifics, yet The deuce a wink of sleep you get! And in a desperate case like this, You ask my best advice:—here ’tis. You know sage R****?—well but stay; So blue you need no look indeed! He wrote a Book—and mind, I say, Do you just get that Book to read.
