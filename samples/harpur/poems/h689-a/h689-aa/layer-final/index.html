[THE WITCH OF HEBRON]

While within A brazen trellis based with slabs of stone And belting all the splendid structure smiled A low-walled Garden, cut with paven paths Into divisions of a hundred forms, Yet all related, and concurring all To form one perfect whole;—aglow throughout With rarest aromatic shrubs, and flowers Effusing odors such as seemed to bring Tidings from Paradise, so rich their fame! And still, at pleasant intervals, were growths Of higher verdure, gemed with golden pips, Or luminous with blossoms, glassed below In tiny fountains raying to the lights Each like a shield of pearl
