A Dilettante

A dilettante, deep in law and politics, Up to all sorts of journalistic jolly tricks, Profoundly far seen in esthetic latitudes, And awfully sage in philosophic platitudes: One of his tastes is, (all his tastes are healthy ones) To toady great men that are also wealthy ones; If very rich, to hug them till they glow he’s fain, But if he finds them poor—he lets them go again.
