Bits

Half and half Freedom.

Freedom that’s half a fact and half a fiction, What is it but a sorry contradiction? Free men are sacred men, not such as may Be ridden rough-shod o’er by those they pay To fight their foes, not them! Yet this might be Decreed and done in any Monarchy! Pure Despotism’s honest at the least, Nor hides the hoof that indicates the beast: But draws from reigning power’s ascendant star The one sole sanction of its Jove-like Czar.
