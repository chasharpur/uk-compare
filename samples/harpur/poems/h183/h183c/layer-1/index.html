Hybrid Freedom

Freedom that’s half a fact and half a fiction, What is it but a sorry contradiction? Free men

are

sacred men; not such as may Be ridden rough-shod oe’r by those they pay To fight their foes—not them. Yet this might be Decreed and done in any Monarchy! But not in a true commonwealth, in which None were a vagabond because not rich; None were a rebel but the traitor wretch Who’d be, for hire, her liberty’s Jack Ketch! And all were tyrants who from power would keep How People, and be-law them into sheep. Pure Despotism’s honest at the least, Nor cloaks the hoof that indicates the Beast; But draws from reigning Power’s ascendant star The one sole sanction of its Jove-like Czar!
