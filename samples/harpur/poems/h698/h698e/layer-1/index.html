A World-Poet.

The ever-influencing Bard is one Whose spirit is warm and genial as the soul Of Summer, when, through nature’s quickened whole, Its teems in fruitage, mellowing ‘neath the sun;— Whose heart doth ever in its fullness run Out tow’rds all beauty and all nobleness, And thence wherever there’s a rich excess In what is suffered, and in what is done. His too is faith—heroic insight;—yea, So broadly doth the Godwardness of man, 120 As an instinctive revelation, ray For him the Future’s providential plan, That even beyond his conscious thought, is he Thy trumpet, Truth! thy prophet, Liberty!
