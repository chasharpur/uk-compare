Love, Friendship, and Faith.

What so sweet upon the earth As Love, when it is new; Or what so golden in its worth, If only Love were true?

What like Friendship for a shield ‘Gainst want, and riches too; Or what such summer fruit might yield, If Friendship were but true?What
