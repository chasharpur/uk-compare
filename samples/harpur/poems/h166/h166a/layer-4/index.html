A Guess at a Why?

A bit of mischief takes with every one of us: And know we truly why, in that we know Its social opposite could be in none of us So much a merit, if it were not so? 423 All being’s thus evinced:—lightness shows weight; Nothing were little but for something great; Nothing were lofty were there nothing low; And even Love, with its celestial glow Finds its extreme proof in the fire of Hate!
