A Guess at a Wherefore?

A bit of mischief’s dear to every one of us: But wherefore? That’s the poser. Because its opposite could be in none of us So much a merit, if it were not so? 423 All being’s thus evinced:—lightness shows weight; Nothing were little were not something great; Nothing were lofty were there nothing low; And even Love, itself is proved by hate.
