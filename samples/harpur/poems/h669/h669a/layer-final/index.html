LAYS FOR THE ANTI-CONVICT ASSOCIATION.

BY CHARLES HARPUR.

No. 1.

[A WAR SONG FOR THE AUSTRALIAN LEAGUE]

Up Australians! hark, the trumpet Calls you to a holy fight! Round the evergreen—your standard Gather, and as one unite! Shall the Mammonists condemn us Into “Felonry” and shame? Or shall truth endiadem us With the stars that write her name?

Shall our matchless southern heaven— Shall our land from verge to verge, Only form a splendid prison For the Felon and his scourge? Rather than a dome for merit— Rather than a region free For the heavenward-soaring spirit Of immortal liberty?

By the virgin soil we’re tilling, A new soul of truth to start; By the blood of honor thrilling In each patriotic heart; By the dignity of Labor, Righteously and nobly free; By the majesty of manhood, That should never, never be!

Jerry’s Plains, 28th September, 1850.
