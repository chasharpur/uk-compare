XX.

Charity.

Man, even in his youth, at heart were hoar, A grinding niggard—the mere slave of Need, Didst thou no influence through his dealings pour. Unmoved by thee; who would, though eloquent, plead Against that scorn which maketh poor indeed The children of distress? Thou art the door Of Wisdom’s social fold, who, all the more The kindless flout thee, holdeth to thy creed. On thy maternal bosom many a time I lay my head, and dream that yet thy reign, In – every earthly clime wide effusion 560 Shall sweeten; and, as o’er some sultry plain Fresh airs breathe vigor, quicken man to attain Capacity for Love’s millennial prime.
