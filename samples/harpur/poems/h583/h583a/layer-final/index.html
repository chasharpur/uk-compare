[...] The following is my latest composition. It was hammered together in head the other day white beating about the Bush for a lot of strayed sheep, and has never until now been put to paper. You will pardon my thus eking out my letter with it. Its text is

All is for the Best.

3

The world is but a boastful weakness— Pale with terrors unconfest; Yet Virtue may not lose her treasure, A God is Power exceeding measure: All then—all is for the best.

The world is mountained o’er with trouble— Cragg’d with danger and unrest; Yet all things tend to the supernal, And God is Peace—yea, peace eternal: All then—all is for the best.

The world is clouded up with error— Folly’s nourished at its breast; Yet even wise men are forecasting, And God is Wisdom Everlasting: All then—all is for the best.

The world is sowed with lies for lucre— Falsehoods all its ways infest; Yet Fraud is overcome by Duty, And God is Truth, and truth is Beauty: 4 All then—all is for the best.

Power, Peace, Wisdome, Truth and Beauty, These are God—and these attest, That Evil must be self-consuming, A bale fire Final Good illuming: All then—all is for the best.
