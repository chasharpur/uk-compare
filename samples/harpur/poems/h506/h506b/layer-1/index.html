VI.

But when they came to the assembled guards With their leaders, none were sleeping found, But all in arms, sat vigilant. As dogs When keeping watch about a sheep-fold, list The furious cries of forest beasts, that come 539 Out from the mountains; while against them still Much clamour is kept up by dogs & men, And sleep is utterly by all foregone; So was sweet slumber to the eyelids lost Of those who during that sad night kept guard, And gazed out tow’rds the darkling plain, and heard The advancing Trojans mustering up unseen.
