[A LYRICAL LOVE STORY 1:5]

[...] In the Being that fills me with rapturous fire As passionate rapture I move! There is majic to her in my name, As hers to my ear is the watchword of Bliss: And what is there else, or in glory or fame, Like this—like this?

I

dream of the beauty and grace Of One who still dreameth of me; I dote on her exquisite—eloquent face Who mine most delighteth to see! Then however lowly my fate, The smiles of true love are all-potent to please: And what is there else, or in riches or state, Like these—like these?)
