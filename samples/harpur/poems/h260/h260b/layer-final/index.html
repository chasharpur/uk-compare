Love Confident.

I’m admired by the One I admire! I am loved by the Maiden I love! In the Being that fills me with rapturous fire, As passionate rapture I move! There’s magic to her in my name, As hers to my heart is the password of Bliss: And what is there else, or in fortune or fame, Like this?

I

dream of the beauty and grace Of One who still dreameth of me! I dote on the charm of her eloquent face Who mine most delighteth to see! Then, however lowly my fate, The smiles of true love shall my future begem: And what is there else, or in riches or state, Like them?
