Mutual Love.

I’m admired by the Girl I admire, I am loved by the Woman I love; In the Being that fills me with rapturous fire, As passionate rapture I move! There is majic to her in my name, As her’s to mine ear is the watchword to Bliss— And what is there else, or in Glory or Fame, Like to this?

I dream of the beauty and grace Of One who still dreameth of me; I dote on her exquisite face Who mine most delighteth to see! Then however dark be my Fate, The smiles of true Love are all-potent to please— And what is there else, or in Riches or State, Like to these?
