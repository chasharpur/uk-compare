Love Confident

I’m admired by the one I admire! I am loved by the maiden I love! In the Being that fills me with rapturous fire, As passionate rapture I move! There is magic to her in my name, As hers to my heart is the password of Bliss: 32 And what is there else, or in fortune or fame, Like this?

I dream of the beauty and grace Of one who still dreameth of me! I dote on the charm of her eloquent face Who mine most delighteth to see! Then, however lowly my fate, The smiles of true love shall my future begem: And what is there else, or in riches or state Like them?
