[AUTUMNAL LEAVES 2]

When The manifold hills, forsaken of the sun, Are duskening into one Featureless mightiness,—how wildly then In the grey solitude of heaven afar There shineth a sole Star! Even so the memory of One adored With all Affection’s hoard Of golden feelings, treasured up for truth In vain—in vain, in pleasure and in pain, Throughout life’s fervent youth, A far bright mystery knowing no remove, Shines—ever shines, peculiar and apart, In the wide vacancy of wasted love— That of a broken heart!
