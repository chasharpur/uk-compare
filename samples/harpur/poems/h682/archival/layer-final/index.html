Why?

Why Heloise—my Heloise! Is Nature thus so rich for me? Is it because my spirit sees Nature through thee?

Why Heloise, seems this wild Bower To me a thing almost divine? Is it because its beauty’s power Is flushed with thine?

Thine, and the spirit it creates, Even Love, that holiest Spirit here, Which, in subduing, elevates, And casts out fear!

In its perfection casts out fear As an infirmity of earth; For Love is triune in its sphere With Faith and Worth.
