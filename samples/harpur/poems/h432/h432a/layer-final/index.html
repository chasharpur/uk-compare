XV.

Rosa compared with the Night.

Oh! lovely as the Night—with all the fires Of Heaven thus burning in the dear expanse, Is Rosa; and even thus her deep eyes glance, Lustrous with glowing thoughts and pure desires, And that divinest pathos which inspires All that’s celestial in young Passion’s trance— All that its mortal music can enhance As with the rapture of Seraphic lyres! I gaze upon her till the atmosphere 52 Sweetens around me with supreme delight! Till all associated forms appear Swimming in bliss, as swim those wonders bright In their ethereal depths—and all I hear Sounds then like silver-wingèd dreams in flight.
