XLIX.

VIRTUELESS PEOPLE.

How many amongst men are those who draw Their breath of life through Custom’s lungs, and deem That right is only something made by law, And all men just as good as they can seem? 115 Morals with such mean—safe things, and they see No worth in worship but—conformity. Yet are they seldom felons: too discreet For theft or robbery—they simply cheat; But this they do to what extent they can, Believing Rascal might be written Man.
