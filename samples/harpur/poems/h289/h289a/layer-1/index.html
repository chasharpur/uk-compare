A wierd

[THE MIDNIGHT MOON IS RIDING HIGH ALOFT]

The midnight Moon is right aloft, Yet only her dim outline may be seen, For the whole heaven is frosted with a soft And creamy cloud; through which her half quencht sheen But filters heav earthward, and, so filtering, seems Light’s wraith, not living light,—so ghostlywise it gleams

A Bit that is sure to tempt the Assaulter of hope and love into an assorted breach of the commandment.
