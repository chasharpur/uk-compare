[RECORDS OF A POET’S LOVE X]

A vast and shadowy hope breaks up my rest, Unspoken; nor dares even my pen to write How pineth my pent spirit, day and night, For one fair Being with whom I might be blest! And ever with vague jealousies possessed, The more I languish, feeling these may so Oppress Affection, that, for very wo, It longs at last to die, deep buried in my breast! O for a brimmer of the wine of Love! Or a deep draught of the Lethèan wave! The power a mutual passion to emove, Or that repose which sealeth up the grave! Yet these my bonds are blameless! One more wise Had dreamt away his freedom, dreaming of her eyes!
