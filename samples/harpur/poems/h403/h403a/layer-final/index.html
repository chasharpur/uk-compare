VIII.

The Pruriency of Dandyism.

Why will you so stare at Beau Prettiman, girls? Already the Creature adores his own charms: 83 Already he vows, while adjusting his curls, That you’re itching—all itching to be in his arms.

Note. I abhor to see, what is but too often seen, in the countenances of handsome youths; namely, the expression of a certain sexual consciousness of their animal beauty. The young smock-faced Priest who was heard to mutter to himself, upon observing that a bevy of high- born dames were regarding his face and figure with evident admiration: “Ay, lust you strumpets!—lust away, and be damned!” was just a forbidden but still prurient Prettiman of this same over-sexually-con- -scious kidney: while x x x, and x x x, are unfortunately unforbidden ones, and every whit as prurient.
