I Would get me a New Love

I would get me a new love, The old fire is out! I would get me a new love Ere Spring comes about! That as in her warm arms The fields all rebloom, My lone heart may awaken From coldness and gloom.

O there’s nothing in nature Like love when ’tis new; And there’s nothing in fortune Like love when tis true! But my passion for Meg, was A fire that’s gone out; And I’d get me a new love Ere Spring comes about.

I would get me a new love, Less custom’s cold slave; And I get me a new love More earnest and brave! Too pure to mistrust me, Too noble to doubt! I would get such a new love Ere Spring comes about.But if

But if such a new love I no-where may get, I’d as lief be as lonely— Nay, lonlier yet! Than have one lik the old love, Just playing a part, With a flare in her fancy, But ice at her heart.
