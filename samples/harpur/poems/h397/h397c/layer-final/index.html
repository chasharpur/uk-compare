7

PRAYER.

Wherever, youth, thy lot may fall, Whatever be thy worldly part, Pray daily to the God of All, ‘Tis needful to the human heart; That grace shall meet thy suppliant call I might affirm, since this I can That prayer unto the God of All, Is sweetening to the life of Man.

Should Evil’s snare thy steps enthrall, And wouldst thou yet her power control, Pray daily to the God of All, ‘Tis leaven to the human soul; That grace shall then attest thy call I might affirm, since this I can That prayer unto the God of All, Is victory to the will of Man.

Charles Harpur.
