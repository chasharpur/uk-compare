The Human Necessity of Prayers

Wherever, Youth thy lot may fall. Whatever be thy moral plan; Pray dayly to the God of All, Tis needful to the heart of Man.

That grace shall meet thy suppliant call I cannot say; but this I can, That Prayer unto the God of All Is sweetening to the heart of Man

Should evil snares thy steps enthrall, And wouldst thou yet reform thy plan, Pray dayly to the God of All, Tis strengthening to the heart of man.

That grace shall then attest thy call I cannot say; but this I can, That Prayer unto the God of All Is victory to the will of Man.
