Never Mind!

My Country! though wild yet, And rude be thy nature, This alone our proud love Should beget and command: There’s room in thy broad breast For Manhood’s full stature, And Honest Endeavour’s A lord in the land!

And though much of thy bounty By aliens in feeling, Has been made upon heads The least worthy to fall, Their reign is nigh past, And the wrong is fast healing, And thy wide arms encircle A home for us all!

And though pygmies high placed In our councils, yet cheat us, In our woods there’s a Giant Upgrowing the while— A man-royal Spirit pre-doomed Pre-doomed to complete us, And cheer on the world From the Great Austral Isle!
