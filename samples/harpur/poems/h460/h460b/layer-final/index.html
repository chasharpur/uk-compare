[Rhymed Cr[i]ticisms, With Prose Notes.]

Praise lyeth wide in Poesy’s dominion, Wherein affection no strait limit knows; Whence in the paths of critical opinion Our Verse should be restricted by our Prose.

I love sometimes in Prose to give The rooted reason of my Rhyme, To show how well the Muse can live On solid Truths, and make them chime.
