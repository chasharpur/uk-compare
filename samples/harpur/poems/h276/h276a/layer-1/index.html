Stanzas.

Let pleasure be thy duty In new love’s sweet success; Be leal to loyal Beauty— But love not to excess! For on whate’er is human Love doats—but to repine, And all that’s bright in Woman Will tarnish and decline.

Yet, if a wild adorer Of something thou must be, Become thee Right’s explorer And Freedom’s devotee; Or if thou wouldst not ever But love unto thy loss, Love thou the God-endeavour Of Jesus on the Cross.
