Capital Punishment.

To string a man up for the very worst crime, Is like smashing a watch for not keeping good time. 102 It were very much wiser, no doubt, To make the wrong watch keep the best time it could, By thoroughly cleaning it out: (What is this but the truth—though in rhyme?) While to use the bad man in a work of some good, Would patent a moral sublime.
