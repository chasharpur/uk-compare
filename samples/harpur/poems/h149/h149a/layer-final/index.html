[...] Behind which, resting it upon the ground, The human warrior standeth wholly hid; Yea, lengthwise ample as a chariot wheel Of massiest structure: thus her fair large face Upturning heavenward, she spake. Her words Broke loudly forth, a mighty utterance,—toned As are the musical but thundrous sounds That issue forth in the recoiling blast From some vast mountain cave; and far away Despread in murmurs through the stormy air; Wild-mixing murmurs, such as Wonder deems The inarticulate prophecies of Fate.
