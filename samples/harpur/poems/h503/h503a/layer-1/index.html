III.

Thus sorely wounded, loud as thunder roared The brazen Mars—loud as ten thousand men 534 Huddled together in the crush of war. So that upon the Greeks and Trojans both Like terrors siezed, as the strife-hungry God Thus bellowed, being wounded. And as when A heavy-blowing wind is loaded dense With gloomy haze engendered by the heat, Such then to Diomed appeared the Form Of brazen Mars, as through the clouds it swept, Ascending swift,—to disappear at last Out in the blue of heaven.
