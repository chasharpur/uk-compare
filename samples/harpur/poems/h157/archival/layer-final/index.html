II.

The Gospel acording to Law.

Constable. I’ve said he’s a Vagrant, and made good what I said, sir, Having proved him too poor to pay for a bed, sir,— An old man with nowhere to lay his old head, sir. Magistrate. Yes, and therefore the Law, as I find it here written, Compels me to lay by the heels this free Briton: A mere Christian might think there must here be some flaw, But such is the Gospel according to Law.
