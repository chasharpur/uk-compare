224

Personal and other Sonnets.

The Same Subject Continued.

For myriads of thy children vainly strive Even for enough of bread, O Italy! Or die in need to keep such shows alive! For not the specious prayer and trumpery fee Of Pope and priest and princely devotee Uphold the cost—but levies on the hive Of grudging Labor while he knaws the gyve Of arm’d Religion, hungering to be free! And free he shall be! Else, O Land of Fire— Land of Volcanoes! is thy modern line Of men resemblant to the Pontine mire, And not to the heaven-reaching Apennine! Not to the storied vales and shores divine That sound of glory like a God-struck lyre!

1850.
