XXIII.

The same Subject continued.

Transcends it?—Yea! For many myriads strive In vain for bread and die in misery, To keep such pious vanities alive. For not the specious prayer and trumpery fee Of pope and priest and princely devotee, Upholds the cost; but levies on the hive Of beggard Labor while he knaws the gyve Of armed Religion, hungering to be free! And free he shall be yet! Else, Land of Fire! Land of Volcanoes! is thy modern line Of men resemblant to the Pontine mire, And not to the storm-baffling Apennine! Not to the storied vales and shores divine, That sound of glory like a God-struck lyre!
