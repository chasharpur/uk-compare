Hope On.

Gold’s a cheat there’s no denying, Love too hath a wandering wing; Still tis useless, useless sighing, Rather list to Hope replying— “Flowers will come again with Spring; And in the desert way we’re going There’s one bounteous blessing flowing— And Hark! I hear the Angel sing.

Fame’s a liar, Beauty fading, Hope itself may plant a sting; Still forbear, forbear upbraiding, Rather list to her pursuading— “Flowers will come again with Spring &c”
