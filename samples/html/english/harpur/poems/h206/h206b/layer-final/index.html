IV.

[JOSEPHINE]

The suns of many years Have rolled in light away, And many a taleful change appears Along the shores of that island-bay. But there is yet one little cove, Round which fond Lovers use to rove, That wears the very look it wore Ere its bright waters flashed before Aught save the Carib’s light canoe. Lake-like, its skiey depths give back, With trembling truth, the flying rack.
