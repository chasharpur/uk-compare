“YES.”

My soul is raying like a star, My heart is happier than a bird, And all to hear through Fortune’s jar One promissory word!

A sound as simple as the low Quick sliding gurgle of a rill, And yet with power to overflow A world with blissful will.

A billow of the summer sea, A cloudlet of the summer sky, How wide is their felicity,— So widely blest am I.

It seems as though the very air Was breathen of the heart of love, As Pleasure in the sun’s bright lair Sat brooding like a dove.

O Beauty, in one little word What boundless power is thine to bless; O Love, a seraph’s voice is heard In thy confiding “Yes!”
