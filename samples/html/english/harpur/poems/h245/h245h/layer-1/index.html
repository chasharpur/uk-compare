Love the Exalter

My soul is raying like a star— My heart is happier than a bird, And all to hear, through Fortune’s jar, One promisory word!

A sound as simple as the low Quick-sliding gurgle of a rill; And yet of power to overflow A world with blissful will!

I feel as if all life & might Were bounding on a loftier course; As if all loveliness and light Were deepened at the source!

I feel as if the very air Was breathen from the heart of Love: As Pleasure in the sun’s bright lair Sat brooding like a dove!

A billow of the sunny sea— A cloudlet of the sunny sky— How wide is their felicity? So widely blest am I!

O Beauty, through one homely word What boundless powers are there to bless! O Love, a seraph’s voice is heard In thy confiding Yes.
