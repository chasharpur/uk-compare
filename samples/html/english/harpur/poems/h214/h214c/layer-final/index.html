THE LAST HOPE.

Wildly upstarting from the bed of care, Which through the long, long night no comfort gave, O where is rest? asked Sorrow in despair, And Hope low answered—In the quiet grave.

Where Scorn forgets her victim to deride, And the bruised heart hath victory over Pain; Where foes, like brothers, lay them side by side, And sundered loving ones are joined again.
