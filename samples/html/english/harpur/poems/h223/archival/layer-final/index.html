“My Life indeed has not been, nor may be From error, folly, and, impatience free, Nor measured to the times’ so cheap morality: Still in each deed designédly put forth 188 To bear into the world a living birth— In such I have walked erect as any man on earth.

x In fine, if men will mistake my character, and I both know and feel that they do so, I must be permitted to express myself accordingly.
