Antipodean Consistency

In England Duffy stormed (or else I dream) Against our Constitution. Our condition As being adverse to it, was a theme On which he out low’d Lowe in opposition: But here he holds it quite a model scheme, The destined engine of a godlike mission! Is this consistent, Sir? Then, if you please, What do we mean by the Antipodes?
