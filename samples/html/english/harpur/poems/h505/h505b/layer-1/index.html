V.

But blameless Gorgythion in the breast Hi arrow struck,—a son of Priam, whom The goddess-like Castianira bore, Being wedded from Gorg. And as when A garden poppy, by its load of fruit And vernal showers together, is weighed down, And droops its head to one side heavily: Even so the head of Gorgythion hung Inclining sideways, by his casque depressed.
