V.

But blameless Gorgythìön in the breast The arrow struck,—a son of Priam, whom The goddess-like Castiänìra bore, Being wedded from Æsỳma. And as when A garden poppy, by its load of fruit And vernal showers together, is weighed down, And droops its head to one side heavily: Even so the head of Gorgythìön hung Inclining sideways, by his casque depressed.
