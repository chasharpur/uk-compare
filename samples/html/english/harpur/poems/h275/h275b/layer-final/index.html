Grief refining into Melancholly.

The Past is flowing through my thoughts— Flowing like a sea; 309 With all its wild waves dancing bright Over what?—an under-might Of darkling loss and misery!

Still it floweth through my thoughts— Moaning like a sea; While of worn Hope I ask alway, Like a dying Cast-away, What can earth’s future be to me?

What can it be to me indeed, But a drearier scene? Or at the very best, sans doubt, But a stale, stale lengthening out Of what the gainless Past hath been!
