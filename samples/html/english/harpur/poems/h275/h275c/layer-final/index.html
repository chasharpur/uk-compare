The Past.

The Past is flowing through my thoughts— Flowing like a sea; With all its billows dancing bright Over what?—an undermight Of darkling loss and destiny.

195

Still it floweth through my thoughts— Floweth like a sea; While of worn Hope I ask alway, Like an unsought cast-astray: What can Earth’s future be to me?

And Hope herself admits: To thee But a dark’ning scene; Or at the very best, sans doubt, But a stale, stale lengthening out Of what the gainless Past hath been!
