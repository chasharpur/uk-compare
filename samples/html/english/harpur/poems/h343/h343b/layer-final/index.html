The Omnipresence of God.

Being a Paraphrase of part of the 139 Psalm.

Whither—whither might I fly From thy presence, God most high? Whither—whither might I go From thy spirit’s overflow? Up to heaven could I rise: Thou art mansioned in the skies! Did I make my bed in hell: Thou wert there—ev’n there as well. Fled I on the morning’s wings— Dwelt I in the deep sea springs: Round me still would work thy will, And thy right hand hold me still. To the dark night should I say Hide me in thy cloud alway: Vain would such a covert be— Darkness is as light to Thee.
