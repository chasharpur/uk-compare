NO. VII.

WENTWORTH AGAIN.

Once he would have the Franchise low—For why? His “brandy faced” supporters then were poor! But these grown rich, (by any means be sure!) He turned his Jacket—and would have it high! Being priced too cheaply by the grasping crew Of Darling’s reign, he was the Many’s man! Now that we’ve something of sage Bentham’s plan To rest in—hark! he’s roaring for the Few! In fine, ’tis thus: that chiefly which is sent forth In public advocacy, to repel The would-be all-in-all ness of one Wentworth, Provokes our Patriot’s rage! And if as well, The Government so operates, it thenceforth Finds “Billy Winford” Faction’s “William Tell.”

AN AUSTRALIAN
