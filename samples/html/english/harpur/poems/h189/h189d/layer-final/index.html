9

IMPROMPTU.

(Composed within the verge of a beautiful bit of woodland scenery.)

This lovely bit of woodland view Idealised supremely In picture, would thenceforth, no doubt, A sweet æsthetic dream be;

With rainbow robe and snowy limb To Fancy’s vision glancing, From yon dim glade, where mythic nymphs Were in the distance dancing.

To measures of celestial tone (For picture can suggest sound)*, Poured forth by festal Gods unseen, All heavened in the back-ground.

But here, amid the actual—ah! It makes the muse feel queerly, To know that out beyond us there We’ve Dullman’s Paddock merely!

His paddock! whose cold presence turns To ice all genial dreaming, And from whose frightful prosiness All poetry flies screaming!

* At least picture does not lack the suggestive power by which the idea of sound is excited in the beholder. One must be very precise and explanatory in these super-critical times.
