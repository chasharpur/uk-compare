Yea, they are clannish, and, like devils, hate Whatever is undonkeyishly great, Or even what’s small, if haply it appears In one unsanctioned by due length of ears!

The one great project born in Donkeydom Whose end and aim is to impede & ban All loftiness of character in Man! Even their giant—the aforesaid Tom— Being a pigmy out of Donkeydom!
