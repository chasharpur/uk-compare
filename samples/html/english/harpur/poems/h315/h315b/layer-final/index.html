11

[LIFE ETHICS]

Be loyal yourself, if you’d prove All the nearness and worth of your mate; For whatever damages love, Is making an inroad for hate.

Be true to your own to the end, And to comfort them never be slow; For whatever weakens a friend, Must strengthen in somewise a foe.

Be firm in your Faith; ’tis a rod Of support in the weariest way: And the life-path that leads you from God, What can it but lead you astray ?
