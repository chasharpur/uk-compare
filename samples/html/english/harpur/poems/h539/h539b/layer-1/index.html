IV.

“Is Saul also among the Prophets?”

“Peace, good Doll! do not speak like a death’s head; do not bid me remember mine end.”

Falstaff.

With ample fortune and no common mind, A heart by nature generous and free, Bland, is it not most pitiful in Thee, To herd with Things as pithless as the wind Even in their noise? To spit thyself abroad With such as spout for patriotic gold Their innate brass—nay, even with such to hold Factious community for Public Fraud? Condemn—abandon them! Being old, be wise; Nor sow thy last few years with moral chaff! Stern watchful Clio has thee in her eyes; And should yon Clique with logic of their lies Mislead thee to the end (though Pride may laugh Now at the threat)—behold your Epitaph!
