To the Same (1867.)

Since thus of thee, a fond paternal strain, To please our household Seven I did devise, 708 Our Charley, by a swift disaster slain, (One of that number) lost in darkness lies! —Doubless his spirit yet loves thee in the skies: But here thy infant wonder asks in vain For one who only in some dream again May seem to watch thee with his gentle eyes! And thou wilt cease to miss him. But for me, So long as yet upon Time’s wasteful shore It may be mine to sojourn (though with thee), Through all the strain of our domestic lore There must be a lone tenor evermore— Like a low wind in our one willow tree.
