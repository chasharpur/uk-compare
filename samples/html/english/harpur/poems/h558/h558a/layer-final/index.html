No. 5.

The Spouse of Infinitude.

Behold, the moon is in the midst of heaven, Serenely shining like a giant pearl Among the fierier jewelry of Night: Of Night, the dark spouse of Infinitude! With inconsumable and boundless beauty, And loveliness—the lovelier because Of its unfathomable mystery— Arrayed as with a robe. O who can look Into her dusk but so resplendent face, As thus, unfailingly recurrent, she Comes over, in her fellowship with Time, Nor feel that she was nourished into being, 5b Upon the bosom of Almighty Power In union with Eternal Verity?
