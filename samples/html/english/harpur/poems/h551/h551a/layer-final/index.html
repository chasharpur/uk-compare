NO. XIV.

A BATCH.

Hannibal!—(Ah! whereto must descend my theme?) D’Arcy and Lawson!—(which the greater spooney?) Wild, Lord, et cetera! each and all as moony In mind and spirit as a drunkard s dream! What! into the unlucky hands of such, My country, should thy interests be given Aught longer? Crudities that satire even Bemuds her fingers having thus to touch! And now, though at some trouble here to rake them Into one Batch, again I let them drift Namelessly off upon the breeze that’s whift From Folly’s wind chest! Let Wentworth make them Just what he can—since only at a gift Faction itself, methinks, can care to take them.

AN AUSTRALIAN.
