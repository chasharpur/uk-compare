Chorus of the Hours.

No rest—no love—is in thy bosom, Earth, For searching natures of the noblest size,— No sane delight—no unadulterate mirth— Nothing that might be an unselfish prize; Because so much monopolised to the use And dissolute abuse Of fortune, accident, and birth, In men nor great, nor good, nor beautiful, nor wise!

And hence thy prime concievers, Through all their course under Time’s clouded skies, Detached in spirit from all actual place, All fitting sympathetic ties, By demon Custom and her dull believers Only in worldly wisdom, glory and grace, Waste but on dreams—out in the Far Away, The infinite riches of their highest love! Or madden with a wild vague sense that they Cannot flee—whither? like the Psalmist’s dove!

Semi Chorus.

When The manifold hills, forsaken of the sun, Are duskening into one Featureless mightiness, how wildly then In the grey solitude of heaven afar There shineth a sole star: Even so the memory of one adored With all affections hoard Of golden feelings, treasured up for truth In vain—in vain, in pleasure and in pain, Throughout Life’s fervent youth, A far bright mystery knowing no remove, Shines—ever shines, peculiar and apart 290 in the wide vacancy of wasted love— The love of a broken heart.

Chorus (in continuation)

Yet shall the morning of a happy change Illumin Hertha’s worn and thoughtful features, Even through the mighty range This thoughtfulness is forced to take By foregone wrong, for redemption’s sake Of her so many heart-uncomforted creations! When Wisdom, dawning time-ward, more and more, Shall purge the world of that mysterious awe Thrown, mist-like, by a bribed Religion o’er The unfraternal policy of the Few: Affirming Love to be the only law Wherewith mankind may righteously be bound! And then, so bounteous as the heaven-shed dew, All healthful joys shall all alike surround, From venemous mixture evermore exempt; Making the near and actual, day by day, Throughout Life’s pleasant way, All Poesie hath hitherto but dreamt As possible in the shadowy Far Away.
