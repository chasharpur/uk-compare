LITERARY REGISTER.

THE SCAMPER OF LIFE.

Avast, ye restless Things! where are you all thus run- ning to? To Nothing? at a pace as though you all were funning too! And with a giddy noise that really is quite stunning too

Crack! there goes J———y L———e, with his donkeys both in hand; And there the Flying Pieman skelters on as loth to stand Which shall be first, I wonder, by and by, in Nothing- Land?

A Beauty scampers past,—a ripe and rosy wonder rare! Perhaps her smiles may buy, if she can keep them fond and fair, A lift o’er the last stile—but not a worm beyond it there!

And whence are yonder Group, with care and scheming pale and bit? Wool-samples in their fists, all eager to make sale of it— I wonder what King Death will offer by the bale for it?

And Pinch’em, after all the bargains thou hast brought about, What shall thou have to do, when life’s poor fight thou’st fought it out, There where no Sugar’s stored, and Tea is never thought about?

And what shall Wentworth’s weigh-bill say, when he’s done sharking here? “Good people there below, don’t let this blade come carking near; He bites; and all he bit went (like B——ȄB———n) bark- ing here!”

And what shall Windeyer’s passport plead for his sad cavilling And patriotic fame, when he too shall have had his fling? “The Bearer did his best—being only formed for badg- ering.”

And when that sage old woman, called by some Old- nick-his-son, Must follow his own pills in patients long since sick and done, Shall Wisdom then, or mere Pretence, have cut her stick and run?

And when “the Doctor” flits, that Northern Saint so prime of wit; Why then the startled earth shall shake with as sublime a fit! As though some Granny’s cat had made at the same time a flit?

And what shall Truth exclaim, when Lowe shall have to draw first near The Schemers of the Past?—”So then he’s off! that Law-nurst seer, Who hated me as much as he feared fighting Broadhurst here!”

Of Cowper?—”There goes he who probed Grant’s qualification so, Being conscious that himself, had scab but brought his Stations low, Was neither qualified by Worth nor Education—no!”

And when great B———n bolts so far, like all too clever men, That his so lovely phiz the world again may never ken, Will Turkey cocks, I wonder, be as proud as ever then?

And W———d and L———d (Arcades ambo), when they trot away, Two biped-bodied Things (like men) shall go to pot that day——— But as to any mind, the World shall lose then—not a ray!

And is it thus, ye Worms, you rush with such grimaces still Tow’rds yonder Brink, all straining for the foremost places still? Yea, though your Fears thereat make the most horrid faces still!

“Then what is Life I cried” said Shelley in the vapours all, And what is Life? say I, if such like are its capers all, And its first fidlers only———Folly’s cat-gut scrapers all

C. H.
