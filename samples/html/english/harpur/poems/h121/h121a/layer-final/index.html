Evasion.

Oh, that I had in my fist but a dagger, Roared Graham, the schoolmaster, gasping for breath; I would drive it right home to my heart, and then stagger, Just like a stuck pig, into darkness and death.

But Old Nick, at his elbow, on hearing this speech, Suggested a fact that soon lessened his swagger; “There’s a sharp pointed knife lying quite within reach”: Yes, said Graham; that’s true;—but a knife’s not a dagger.
