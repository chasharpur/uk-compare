101

EVA GRAY.

Paler, paler, day by day, Waxeth wordless Eva Gray, Wasting through the heart away!

102

How shall those who wish her well, Lift the shadow erst that fell Round her from Love’s darken’d spell?

Would they have her feast with Song? Ah! its voices but prolong Like far echoes Memory’s wrong.

Would they to beguile her leisure Sweet reliefs in music measure? Music dreams of foregone pleasure.

Would they lure her where the spring Gives the unshadow’d heart to wing Upward like a bird and sing?

Still paler, paler, day by day, Waxeth gentle Eva Gray,— Wasting like a cloud away.
