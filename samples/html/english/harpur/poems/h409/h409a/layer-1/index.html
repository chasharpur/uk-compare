Paraphrase of the 113 Psalm.

Praise God, ye servants of his will, And magnify his name! From everlasting hill to hill Resounded be the same!

Ye nations, pour abroad this praise 12 And wonder of his might! From sunrise to the sunset ‘blaze His glory’s living light!

Though with eternal power arrayed, Yet he in mercy can Descend, even to the earth, to aid So weak a thing as man.

Or from the dust to lift his head Whose lot may lowliest be; And fill with gladness and with bread The child of poverty:

Or to exalt him, till he stand O’er men of Kingly birth, And rule with a superior hand The mighty of the earth.

He wills it, and the barren womb Conceives the child of grace, To fill the else unfruitful house 13 With utterers of his praise.
