To be added to the Spirit of Beauty

(A prophesy)

And in this Sothern Land there yet shall be A race begotten in the spirit of beauty, Such as the olden Greeks were, limb’d and shaped By that deep Ideality which works Into the stuff of Nature, and becomes Progressively its mould; and in and through The physical perfection manifest, Shall burn a soul of power surpassing that Which was in Greece only the effluence Of an artistic, not an actual life. But here it shall be Actual—making all Man’s with his motions instincts modulate, Till thus perfectionized, his native growth Embody forth the Living Beautiful.
