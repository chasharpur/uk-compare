Trust in God.

Deep trust in God! for that I still have sought Through all the dim doubts that beshade the soul, When in the amazement of far-reaching thought We list the thunders that for ever roll Out of that clouded lair Where this world’s Destiny doth its secrets keep. And when I’ve stood upon some fearful steep Of speculation, that did heave its base into the nebulous air Of endless chance, and thence tremendously Threw its dark jaggëd shadow, like despair, Into the dread Unknown.—Deep trust in thee, O God, hath been my object even there.
