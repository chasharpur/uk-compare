Trust in God.

Deep trust in God! for that I still have sought Through all the dim doubts that beshade the soul, When in the amazement of far-searching thought We list the thunders that for ever roll Out of that clouded lair Where this world’s Destiny doth its secrets keep. And when I’ve stood upon some fearful steep That heaved its ridge into the starry air Of Vision, looking forth tremendously Into the dread unknown.—Deep trust in thee, O God, hath been my object even there.
