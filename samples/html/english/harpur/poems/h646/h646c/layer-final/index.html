Trust in God.

Deep trust in God!—for that I still have sought Through all the dread doubts that beshade the soul, When, in the amazement of far-reaching thought, We list the laborings that for ever roll Their thundrous wheels within those clouded regions Where Night and Destiny the counsels keep Of Time, developing his shadowy legions. And when I’ve stood upon some fearful steep Of Speculation,—heaving up its bare And rugged ridge into the nebulous air Of endless change, and thence tremendously Throwing its shadow, like a blind man’s stare, Into the dread Unknown,—Deep trust in thee, O God, hath been my refuge even there.
