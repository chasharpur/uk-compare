13

131st. Psalm

O Lord, no more my heart is proud, No longer lofty are mine eyes; Nor do I now in things too high My spirit exercise. But all my hope and trust in thee, Henceforward shall forever be.

From all that once disquieted My froward soul, with pride beguiled, Have I not weaned it now, O Lord, Even as a weanëd child? And all my hope and trust in thee Henceforward shall forever be.
