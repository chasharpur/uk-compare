ON THE DEATH OF AN INFANT RELATIVE.

And art thou gone—so early gone! dear Child? But yesterday, and thou amidst us grew Fresh as a breeze upon the mountains wild, Bright as a blossom bathed in morning dew: Another day is past—and thou art found A mouldering fragment of the common ground.
