XXV. X

Self Dependence.

I who write this have practised what I preach, Have the hard road of Self-dependence trod: And all who would a growth heroic reach Must in themselves trust only—under God!And 258 And what a noble thing is labor, which For manly freedom manfully undergone, O’er all detraction (while it maketh rich) Will give us victory though we stand alone! Depend not upon friends. I once believed In many, and I yet believe in one: But why should we much risk our being deceived? Why trust until our social faith’s undone? Still, thou thus self dependent, tow’rds our kind Bear we at least a Charitable mind.
