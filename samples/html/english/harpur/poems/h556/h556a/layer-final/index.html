SONNETS DEDICATED TO THE SQUATOCRACY.

NO. I.

Why should hired laborers have Tea allowed? Seeing they only have a right to eat Because they could not work else! Being a treat But fit for gentlemen, Tea makes them proud, And thence offensive to their Masters all! And to offend either in meat or drink Affects Religion—See the Text; I think It will be found in the Apostle Paul. But what if our Stock-horses look’d for Tea? Egad, we’d wonder! and for aught I see, Servants are just as much their Masters’ Cattle During their term of hire, or ought to be! Thus should the case stand, howe’er some may prattle, In sound home practice—curse all Theory!

A SQUATTER OF THE FIRST WATER.
