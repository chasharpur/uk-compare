Mr. Frank Fowler’s editorial and sponsorial connection with “Life and Love”, and other like effusions, induced me at the time to foolscap him with the following jeu d’ esprit

A critic at the top of his ladder.

Every sect will have its canters, Every stage will have its ranters; Every craft will have its mystic Doctors, greyly cabalistic; Every art will have its riddlers, Jargoning like drunken fiddlers: Whence it happens, one Frank Fowler, With his constant cheek-by-jowler, Namby Pamby, for his very Clever under-secretary, 278 Is the self-installed Grand Vizier Of the Doggreldom we quiz here.
