30

Vanished Away.

Heaven’s face was starred as now, When homeward, long ago, I trod: Blest!—having won my Dora’s vow To love me next to God! And as homeward then I went Upon that happy far night, Up into the firmament My glowing spririt rayed, and lent Even to the star-light.

Years are gone—and Dora now Is gone, too, under the green sod: No!—gone to Paradise with her vow To love me next to God! 31 And as here aloft I gaze As on that happy far night, Heaven’s face is all a-blaze, And up my spirit, yearning, rays— But above the star-light.
