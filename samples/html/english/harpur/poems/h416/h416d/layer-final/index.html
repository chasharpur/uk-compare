VI.

Reasons for a Red Nose.

Ask the Doctor why Mrs. G’s nose is so red? And “brandy”’s the word /with a wink/ that is said. But the Lady herself says she certainly thinks, ‘Tis because of the great deal of “water” she drinks. Now neither is quite right, nor wrong, in the matter, Since the truth lies between them: ’tis brandy and water.
