Love, Friendship, and Faith.

What so sweet upon the earth As young Love’s honey dew, Or what so wealthy in its worth, If Love were only true?

What like Friendship for a shield ‘Gainst want and riches too, Or what such summer fruit might yield, If Friendship were but true?

What like Faith in God that brings The gates of heaven to view, With Love and Friendship for its wings, If all alike were true?

All alike of trusty stuff! And why are they not so? There still were room, and room enough For Misery here below.
