Love, Friendship and Faith.

What so sweet upon the earth As love, when it is new? Or what so priceless in its worth, If Love were only true?

What like Friendship for a shield ‘Gainst want and riches too? Or what such summer fruit might yield, If Friendship were but true?

What like Faith, whose vision brings The gates of heaven in view, With Love and Friendship for its wings, If all alike were true?
