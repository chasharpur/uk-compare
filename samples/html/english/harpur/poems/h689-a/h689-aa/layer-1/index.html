[THE WITCH OF HEBRON]

While within A brazen lattice based on polished stone, slabs A garden, belting the which structure A pleasant Garden, cut with paven paths Into divisions of a hundred forms, Yet all related, answering each to each, To form one beautiful whole;—aglow throughout With rarest aromatic shrubs, and flowers Effusing odors such as seemed to bring Tidings from Paradise, so rich their fame! And mixed at intervals with these, were trees And still, at happy intervals, were growths Of higher verdure, gemed with golden pips, Or luminous with blossoms, glassed below In tiny fountains raying to the lights Each like a shield of jasper
