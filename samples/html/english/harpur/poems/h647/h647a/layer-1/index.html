Truth should be dearer than Belief.

A principle is refutation-tight, When proved quite natural—and therefore right. The We’re simply hurt by one who won’t believe us, But we abhor the man who would deceive us! And rightly; for the matter standeth thus: That doubts our truthfulness, but this through us Would cozen Truth her self! And hence my Brief; —That Truth should be dearer than Belief;—
