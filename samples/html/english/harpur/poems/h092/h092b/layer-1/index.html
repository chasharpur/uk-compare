7.

Descriptive of the Prospect from Mount View

Hurrah!—Below—around me—in one view What regions spread!—Here Painting would but mar Westward, behold the hills that hide afar The Hunter’s source, now blending with the blue Of heaven as waves with the horizon do. Thence North, and Eastward, mightier ranges bar The prospect—even to where the sun’s red car Comes wheeling daily, to his season true. And in the valleys of the streams that steal Along the foldings of the mountains, lo, Civilization oft hath set her seal, That Plenty in the opening might grow— But, drunk with the huge scene, my senses reel, And Horror yawns in the green gulfs below.
