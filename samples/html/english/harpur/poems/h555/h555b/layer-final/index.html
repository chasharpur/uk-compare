2d

III.

THE SPIRIT OF LOVE.

(From King Saul.)

When broad o’er her hill top Hangs Eve’s glowing star, A spirit of beauty Then comes from afar; To dwell with the blossoms That people the trees, And stray with the odours That passion the breeze;

To mellow the pipings That come from the hills, Where the shepherds are folding Their flocks by the rills, In the wild wood to deepen The doves gentle voice, To live in all life, In all gladness rejoice:—

It is breathing around, It is glowing above; That spirit of beauty— The Spirit of Love.
