Nature’s apparent Want of Benevolence

The man by indigestion smitten Finds poison even in his gruel; The man compelled to drown a kitten Must feel that Natures sometimes cruel.

Yet, in both cases, could we see The final reason, we should find (Of this I feel assured) that she Is cruel only to be kind.
