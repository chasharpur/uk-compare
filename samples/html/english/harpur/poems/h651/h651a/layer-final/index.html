Twenty to one.

My Zoilus, one unlucky day, Being puzzled sorely what to say Ventured on this: “His verse is all Essentially unmusical.” The sentence is obscure, no doubt, Perhaps means nothing when made out: But passing that,—if in this curt Effusion I should now assert, ‘Tis thus far provable at once That he’s essentially a dunce! For one that did with him agree, Would not full twenty go with me?
