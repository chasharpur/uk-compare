A Portion of Charles Harpur’s Wild Bee of Australia.

Alteration in the concluding lines of the Flight of Peace.

[...] Dwelling there with Faith, and viewing God through all things—all renewing. x

x In the earlier publications of this Poem it ended as follows,

Dwelling there with Faith, and seeing God’s right hand all things decreeing.

But these lines were faulty in their logic. To say that God decrees all things is to attribute evil to his moral Government of the world. But being omnipotent, he must overrule all things; and being omniscient also, he must overrule them for good. Hence the present reading.
