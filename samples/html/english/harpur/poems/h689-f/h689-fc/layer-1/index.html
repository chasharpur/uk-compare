[FRAGMENT FROM THE WITCH OF HEBRON].

That filled me with much thankfulness of heart.

‘Twere long to tell what haps befell me next. I was the captive of a Bedoine shiek, And fed his camels while I served his lusts; Was sold in the slave mart of Astrachan And carried thence to India, to be crowned A Rajahpoot’s sultana; from which state Flying at length, I fled into a worse Being pounced on by a Turcoman horse-stealer— At once his mistress and his water-carrier. At Adrianople I became the slave Of a harsh Roman matron, who was wont To flog and famish me to make me good, And when I owned myself converted, then She flogged and famished me the more, to make My goodness lasting. But this mush of change In the contexture of my fatal life But troubles and distracts me. The extremes Is what I best remember: I was now A queen, and now a drudge: and finally Fell stabbed in Cairo—slaughtered by a slave.
