AUSTRALIAN LYRICS.—No. 1.

THE BOWER.

Idalia, in thy favorite shade A pleasant bower I’ve made, That thou, what time the sun is high, Mayst shroud thee from the casual eye, And by the bright brook gushing by O’ersprinkled be: Emblem of purity and thee.
