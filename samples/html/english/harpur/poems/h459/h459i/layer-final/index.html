[A RHYME]

I clap to the temper of my Lyre Up rushing wings of fire! Or more to keep the sensual under Lap it in soul-shaking thunder! While for the noble and the wise, The valiant and the free, I sing of that millenial sunrise Of hardy progress destined yet to be! When all true men shall seek, in foul & fair, To breathe with lion lungs the difficult mountain air Of strenuous, rough, upclimbing Liberty! [...]
