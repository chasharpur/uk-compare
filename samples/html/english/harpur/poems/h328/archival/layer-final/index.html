15.

Natural Piety

Give me to wander oft, in lone devotion, Under the round and moonless Heaven! when The stars, thick isling their ethereal ocean, Burn all in undiminished state! Oh, then Rapt from all earthly passion—then I feel Even from my Spirit’s inmost fountain streaming Untaught Religion! streaming to reveal With an assurance there is no misdeeming, That I’m authentic by as bright a seal As stampt those Orbs into eternal beaming! Yes; in the ample volume of the skies Reading of Immortality, elate On Faith’s expanded wings, I learn to rise Above injurious Time and all the wrongs of Fate.
