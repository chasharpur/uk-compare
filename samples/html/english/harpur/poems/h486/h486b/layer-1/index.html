Where, first the first broad shade, a dappled Cow Stoops her meek head over the grassy brink, And then, with rapid and strong gulphs, indraws The pure cool lymph: and though at length sufficed, Still over it she keeps her neck declined And breathes her herby breath upon the water, Ruffling the Image of herself below, As there, with dappled sides, It stands reversed: Herself and Shadow, both Patient expressions of her utter comfort.
