XXXIV.

The same Subject continued.

For myriads of thy children vainly strive Even for enough of bread—O Italy! To keep such pious vanities alive! For not the specious prayer and trumpery fee Of Pope and priest and princely devotee, Uphold the cost;—but levies on the hive Of grudging Labor while he knaws the gyve By armed Religion clenched, as hungering to be free! —And free he shall be! Else, O Land of fire! Land of volcanoes! is thy modern line Of men resemblant to the Pontine mire, 47 And not to the heaven-reaching Apennine! Not to the storied vales and shores divine, That sound of glory like a God-struck lyre!
