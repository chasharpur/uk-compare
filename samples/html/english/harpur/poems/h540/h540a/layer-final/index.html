SONNETS DEDICATED TO AUSTRALIAN SENATORS.

NO. V.

Like Cases, with a Dose of Physic.

John Wroe* mistook a drumming emptiness In’s spiritual bowels, for a call To play the Seer; and N————, no less Art thou mistaken, when, with mind so small Thou dar’st in Legislative strains to bawl? Nay more so; seeing, that with this, in thee A bastard pride abhors that Liberty Should be the heritage alike of all! No doubt, thine hand’s obstetrically skilful, And potent to unload a desperate feeder; Nevertheless, though sick, Australia’s wilful, And begs thee, as a Doctor, not to heed her! Poor Wench! already of much quackery pill-full, She greatly fears some scheme’s afloat to ‘bleed’ her.

ANTI-STATE-PHLEBOTOMY.

* It will be remembered that some time ago, this “New Light” of the Old World paid an antipodean visit to our southern Metropolis.
