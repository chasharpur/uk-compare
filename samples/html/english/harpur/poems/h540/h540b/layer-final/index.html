V.

Like Cases, with a Dose of Physic.

“O, wad some Power the giftie gie gie us To see oursels as others see us, It wad fra mony a blunder free us, And foolish notion.”

John Wroe mistook a drumming emptiness In’s spiritual bowels, for a call To play the Seer—and Nicholson, no less Art thou mistaken when with mind so small Thou darest in Legislative strains to bawl! Nay, more so; seeing that with this in Thee A bastard pride abhors that Liberty Should be the heritage alike of All! No doubt your hand’s obstetrically skilful, And potent to unload a desperate feeder too: Nevertheless, though sick, Australia’s wilful, And begs, Sir, as her doctor, not to heed her you: Poor Wench! already of much quackery pill-full, She greatly fears some Scheme’s afoot to bleed her too.
