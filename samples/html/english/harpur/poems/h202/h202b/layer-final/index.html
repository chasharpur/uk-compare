93

V.

JOB A REVIEWER.

“Oh, that mine enemy would write a book!” Cried Job: and, doubtless, even in these our days, Such were the very deed whereon to hook Our damning censure or more damning praise! (The last mode being the best way to cook An enemy’s goose out of a hundred ways:) And hence the wish makes one feel almost sure That Job had tried his hand as a Reviewer.
