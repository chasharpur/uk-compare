Compensation. X

A sylphic form and angel face, A young life void of sin— The outward liniaments and grace Of purity within: Such was my spirit’s visioned joy When I was a dreaming Boy.

A figure rich in pleasant health, A warm heart and a good— The summer attributes and wealth Of thorough Womanhood: 123 Such was my spirit’s yearned-for truth When I was a lonely Youth.

And in my Manhood it may be I have not chanced to find Even One who realised to me The Lady of my mind; Yet therefore feel I not as crost— Even that sweet Dream’s unlost.

While in the power that dreamt there lives A love-creating ray Worth all that fortune ever gives, Or ever takes away; Inspiring oft a genial rhyme, Even ’mid the snows of Time.
