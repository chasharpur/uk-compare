Epigram.

Dan, turned insolvent, swore thus—Blood an’ ‘ounds! Why, Harry, should our friendship now be sundered? If I have diddled you of twenty pounds— What’s that?—I’ve diddled Roger of a hundred! And this with Hal, though said without compunction, Had, strange to say! a mollifying unction.
