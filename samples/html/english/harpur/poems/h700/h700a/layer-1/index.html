A World-wide surety

Address thyself right manfully to what Lies nearest to thee in the path of duty: And never doubt thou of that meet reward Which comes, by open or by secret ways, Announced or unannounced, to all right doing, Through those fine compensations that descend From God’s paternal hand, down into Nature; Making her years—the showery and the dry— Summer and Winter—full of harvest days: While the mere consciousness of having well Fulfilled our plan in the great march of life, Is spiritual gold,—a standard value That nothing can depreciate or alloy.
