II

A World Wide Surety.

Address thyself right manfully to what Lies nearest in the obvious paths of Duty, And never doubt thou of that meet reward Which comes, by open or by secret ways, Announced or unannounced, to all right doing, Through those fine compensations that descend From God’s paternal hand, down into Nature; 496 Making her years—the showery and the dry— Summer and Winter—full of harvest days: While the mere consciousness of having well Fulfilled our mission in this working world, Is spiritual gold,—a standard value That nothing can depreciate or alloy.
