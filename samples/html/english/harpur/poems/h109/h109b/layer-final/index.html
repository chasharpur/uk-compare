XII.

A SCHOOL MOTTO.

I beat you for your good! The master cries: Then spare me for your own! The boy replies. There’s reason here on both sides; for at school, The mean (as elsewhere) is the golden rule. Undue severity but hardens more; And Birch’em’s temper may be even more sore Than Dunce’s buttocks, ere the flogging’s o’er.
