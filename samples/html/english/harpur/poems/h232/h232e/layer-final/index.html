Lyrics.

Love.

O love is passing sweet, When ’tis ours indeed to meet With a golden hearted Maid, Nothing loth, and not afraid To indrink its Orphic fire, Till her whole soul, like a lyre, Thrill and beat To the thought, and with the feeling,— Love is sweet.

‘Tis like a sunset sky Blushing beauty, to the eye; It is like a summer wind Breathing odors, to the mind; It is like the fervent roll Of rich music to the soul; And we cry Let us always love, or failing,— Let us die!
