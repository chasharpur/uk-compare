Love. X

Oh, Love is passing sweet, When we meet With a golden-hearted Maid, Not afraid To indrink its Orphic fire, Till her whole soul, like a lyre, Thrill and beat To the thought, and with the feeling,— Love is sweet.

‘Tis like a sunset sky To the eye; It is like an odorous wind To the mind; It is like the fervent roll Of rich music to the soul— And we cry Let us always love, or failing,— Let us die!
