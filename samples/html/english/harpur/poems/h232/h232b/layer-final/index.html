SONG.

O Love is passing sweet, When we meet With a golden-hearted Maid, Not afraid So to glory in her part, As to love with all her heart, Till it beat—till it beat To the nature-warbled tune of—Love is sweet.

‘Tis like a sunset sky To the eye; It is like an odorous wind To the mind; It is like the fervent roll Of rich music to the soul, And we cry—and we cry Let us always love, or failing,—let us die!
