IV.

Emblems.

A Streamlet is a bright and beautiful creature, In some wide desert, where it keeps a part Of each Wayfarer’s heart: The Star of Evening is a gracious feature, Instinct, as ‘twere, with all the soul that eyes Have poured against the skies.

And hence, one emblems, in its beautiful being, The memory of a Joy that in my heart Flowed gratefully apart: The other, to my spiritual seeing, The perpetuity of Love’s white grace Yet in some heavenly place.
