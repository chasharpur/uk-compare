Emblems.

A Streamlet is a bright and beauteous creature In some wide desert, where ’tis blest the more In way-worn Memory’s lore; The Star of Evening is a gracious feature Instinct, as ‘twere, with all the love that eyes Have ever looked through, looking at the skies.

And hence One emblems in its beauteous being The memory of a love that in my heart Flowed gratefully apart; The Other to my spiritüal seeing, The perpetuity of that love’s white grace Yet, as a radiance, in some heavenly place.
