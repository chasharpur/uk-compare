IV.

Emblems.

Finally Copied

A Streamlet is a bright and beautiful creature, In some wide desert where it keeps a part Of each wayfarer’s heart; The Star of Evening is a gracious feature, Instinct, as ‘twere, with all the love that eyes Have ever looked through, looking at the skies.

65

And hence one emblems in its beautiful being, The memory of a love that in my heart Flowed gratefully apart; The other, to my spiritual seeing, The perpetuity of that love’s white grace Yet, as a radiance, in some heavenly place.
