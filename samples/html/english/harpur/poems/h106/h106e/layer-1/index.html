O there is a conscious smiling In a heaven so clearly blue; And it must be a felt joyfulness That thus comes blooming through Great Nature’s mother-heart, where— The golden year is new!

In the Early Summer season, When the golden year is new When the woods are whitened over By the jolly cockatoo When wide warm fields are glad with corn As green as ever grew, And upland growths of wattles Engolden all the view!
