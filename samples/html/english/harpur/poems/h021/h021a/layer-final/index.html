[AUTUMNAL LEAVES 2]

The manifold hills, forsaken of the sun, Are dusking into one Featureless Mightiness gloomed up with dun, And in the solitude of heaven afar There shineth a sole star: Even so the memory of one adored With all Affection’s hoard Of golden feelings treasured up for truth In vain throughout our youth, A far bright mystery, still shines apart O’er the wide vacancy of Love’s lone heart!
