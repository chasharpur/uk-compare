But when they came to where the assembled guards Watched with their leaders, none were sleeping found, But all,—in arms,—sat vigilant. As dogs When keeping watch about a sheep-fold, list The furious cries of forest beasts, that come 535 Out from the mountains; while against them still Much clamor is kept up by dogs and men, And sleep was utterly a thing

: So was sweet slumber to the eyelids lost Of those who during that sad night kept guard, And gazed out tow’rds the darkling plain, and heard The advancing Trojans mustering up unseen.
