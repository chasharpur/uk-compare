[THE PANG OF DEATH]

How great or small may be the pang of Death, No mortal knows, or can know, while the breath Of Life he breathes: of all beneath the sky, Only the dead know what it is to die. Nay, so minute, so fine, the point may be Whereat Time ends—out in Eternity, That ev’n the parted Ghost can not decide As to the moment when its body died.
