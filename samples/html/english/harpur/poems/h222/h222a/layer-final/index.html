5d

II.

Lines suggested by a newly-invented kind of Pickle-Dish.

Well done, Improvement! thou dost even out-race Our most fantastic, most fastidious wishes; Since that for serving-up with better grace Even pickles, we have now these special dishes!

But while I scan them with admiring glance, They breed a thought, that stings me like a prickle, Asking Improvement, if by any chance, Pickle-Dish makers ever eat a pickle?

“Oh no! not they! those pungent belly plasters, Are only for their “pastors and their masters,” Answers Improvement; and, to stare off schism, The well-fed hussy quotes Church Catechism.
