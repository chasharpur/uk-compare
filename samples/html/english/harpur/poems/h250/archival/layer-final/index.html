Love Vindicated.

“The life and soul of Love Is sweet Variety.”—Lord Lansdowne.

By the good Love can only be felt, and but for the good; Hath its death, not its life, in Variety: And its soul are benevolence, chastity, fortitude, Fidelity, patience, and piety.

And be certain, that all who have sought it through vanity In a yet unexhausted Variety. Have been spoiled of the holiest joy of humanity By shame, and disgust, and satiety.
