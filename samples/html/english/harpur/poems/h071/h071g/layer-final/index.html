The Combat.

A trooper of France, in desperate need Had struggled from under his dying steed, Where Egypt’s pyramids appeared; Whilst on his black war-horse the Turk Who had borne him down, to finish his work Back wheelingly careered.

Taking his resolute stand Foot-deep in his charger’s blood, The soldier of Christendom, sword in hand, That, gleamingly out pointing, showed Like the lightning tongue of a settling cloud, His foe awaiting stood
