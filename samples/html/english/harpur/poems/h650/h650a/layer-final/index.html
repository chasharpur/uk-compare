5b

[THE TRUTH OF NATURE]

The frame of the World to its Maker is true, And the foul but a means for ensuring the fair: The storms that submerge a whole navy, renew, As by torment, the health of the ocean and air. A shower too abundant may give me to rue, Yet whole Lands are enriched by what’s hurtful to me. Nay, even the serpent has something to do In Nature that’s needful—whatever it be.
