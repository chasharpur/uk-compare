X.

THE FIRST GREAT AUSTRALIAN POET.

Glorious his lot whom Poesie shall name Her first High Priest in this so sunny clime, Though thereby clothed as with a robe of flame!— With her Creations of the Olden Time Much conversant, and by their bulk sublime Moulding new matter, let him build to Fame; Quarrying from Nature’s everlasting frame The sculptured beauty of his lofty Rhyme! Then mirrored ever in his polished page Shall glow his Countrywomen’s lustrous eyes; And future Patriots a righteous rage Thence catch or stimulate—the Brave, and Wise, And Lovely, so, beneath his native skies, Hallowing his Memory from age to age.
