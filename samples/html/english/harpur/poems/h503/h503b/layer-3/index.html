Thus sorely wounded, loud as thunder roared The brazen Mars—loud as ten thousand men 538 Huddled together in the crush of war. So that upon the Greeks & Trojans both Like terrors siezed, as the strife-hungry God Thus bellowed, being wounded. And as when A heavy-blowing wind is loaded dark With gloomy haze engendered by the heat, Such then to Diomed appeared the Form Of brazen Mars, as through the clouds it swept Ascending into the broad blue of heaven
