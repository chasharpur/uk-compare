True and False Glory.

How long shall splendid guilt mankind dec? See furious Conqueror after Conqueror sweep Over the Past! ambitious each to achieve Unequalled sway unrightious victory, and so reap Unequalled sway, and make their age one heap Of kindless triumphs for slave-bards to weave Into loud verse; and finally, so leave To Glory names that Infamy should keep! How greater they, who tyrants did debel, Putting yet all personal dominion down, But such as were the means to serving well? Compare an Alexander’s wild renown With those immaculate memories that crown The lives of Hampden, Washington, and Tell!
