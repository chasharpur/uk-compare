LV.

Glory Tested.

In splendid wrong how pronely men believe! See furious Conqueror after Conqueor sweep Over the Past! ambitious each to achieve Time’s most unrighteous triumph, and so leap To Empire!—making every age a heap Of bloody marvels for slave-bards to weave Into loud-sounding verse, and thereby leave To Glory names that Infamy should keep! How greater they, who Tyrants did debel, Putting all personal dominion down, Save as a master means to serving well? Compare the Macedonian’s wild renown With those rare memories that like amaranths crown The deeds of Hampden, Washington, and Tell!
