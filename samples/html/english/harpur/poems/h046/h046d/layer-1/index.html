BISHOP BERKEELY.

I love him mightily, and fain would know Whither he’d have me, or not have me, go; Yet read to find him too recòndite, since His logic puzzles so, it don t convince. So wide his arguments, that we suspect them Of aberrations though we can’t detect them. Splendid as meteors on their flaming way, They light us heavenward—till we feel astray; Or else, like halcyons, in a shining train, So deep they dive into the mighty main X 110 Of Reason,—they can ne’er come up again!
