Rhymes, Humourous Poems, Epigrams &c.

Bishop Berkeley.

I love him mightily, and fain would know Whither he’d have me, or not have me, go: Yet read to find him too recondite, since 398 His logic puzzles so, it don’t convince. So wide his arguments, that we suspect them Of aberrations, though we can’t detect them. Splendid as meteors on their flaming way They light us heavenward—till we feel astray; Or else so deep they dive into the main Of reason, that they ne’er come up again.
